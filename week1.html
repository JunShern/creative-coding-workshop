Week 1

Do first, think later

This week is all about getting our hands dirty, diving right into the code to make things, break things, and get things we didn’t expect!

You’ll build your first “Hello, World!” program, and add your own spin to it to make it more crazy and more colorful.

In the process, we’ll look at some of the fundamental concepts in programming including variables and loops, applied to a computer graphics environment.

Project: Uncovering "Hello, World!"
