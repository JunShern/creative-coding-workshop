Week 2

How to be lazy

Let's be real. Programmers are just people who work really hard to be lazy.

In this session, we'll look at programs that make decisions for us using conditionals, and try to reduce the amount of typing we need to do using functions.

Finally, we'll use everything we've learnt so far to build a voice-controlled game of Pong. How lazy can you get?!
